ProcrastiPet

Turn your focus sessions into a pet collection.

Android

The Problem

Most productivity apps treat focus like a chore. You set a timer, stare at a countdown, and feel guilty every time you break it. The discipline is all stick, no carrot.

Procrastination isn't a willpower failure — it's a motivation problem. When the reward for finishing a task is just… the next task, it's hard to start at all.

The Solution

ProcrastiPet adds a tangible reward loop to the Pomodoro technique. Complete a focus session and you earn a random pet — common, rare, or legendary. Your collection grows with your focus streak.

It keeps everything on your device. No account required, no notifications from a server, no data leaving your phone. The gamification is lightweight and honest — it's there to help you build a habit, not to hook you into the app.

Key Features

⏱️
Focus Timer Configurable Pomodoro-style sessions with custom durations and break reminders.
🐾
Pet Collection Earn a random pet after each session. Common, rare, epic, and legendary tiers.
🔥
Daily Streaks Track your daily focus habit and protect your streak with consistency.
🏆
Achievements Unlock milestones for total sessions, streak lengths, and rare pet discoveries.
🎯
Daily Challenges Optional daily goals that reward bonus tokens for completing extra sessions.
🔔
Local Notifications Streak reminders and session nudges, scheduled entirely on your device — no server involved.
🔒
Private by Design No account, no analytics, no ads. All data stays on your device. Export / import any time.
🌗
Light & Dark Mode Follows system theme or set it manually. Works comfortably at any time of day.
